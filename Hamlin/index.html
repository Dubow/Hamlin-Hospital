    Our Services

    Critical Care Unit

    At Hamlin Hospital, our state-of-the-art Critical Care Unit is equipped with advanced technology and staffed by experienced professionals to provide care for critically ill patients, ensuring the highest level of attention and treatment.

    Inpatient Services

    Inpatient Services we offer include Maternity, pediatric, and Medical/Surgical Services. The wards are equipped to enhance comfort and quick recovery for patients. The Wards setup is also more convenient and comfortable for inpatient clients.

    Outpatient Services

    Outpatient Services are available 24 hours. The Hospital has an outpatient care facility that includes primary care clinics, community health clinics, urgent care centers, specialized outpatient center, pharmacy, and emergency rooms.

    Theatre Services

    Hamlin Hospital boasts modern, fully equipped theaters where our skilled surgical team performs a wide range of surgical procedures, adhering to the highest standards of safety and precision to promote a successful and comfortable recovery.

    Pharmacy Services

    Hamlin Hospital offers 24-Hour pharmaceutical services with a wide range of medicines available, staffed by highly qualified professionals, and takes commitment in offering first-rate services to both in-patients and out-patients and ensuring that treatment is safe and at the best price possible.

    Laboratory Services

    Our hospital features an advanced laboratory facility with cutting-edge equipment and trained technologists. We conduct a comprehensive array of diagnostic tests swiftly and accurately, aiding in timely diagnosis and effective treatment planning.



    Maternity Services

    Experience exceptional maternity care at Hamlin Hospital. Our dedicated team of obstetricians and specialized facilities ensure a safe and comfortable environment for expectant mothers, supporting them throughout their pregnancy and childbirth journey.
